About

My journey as an artist and a developer.

2007

Discovering Digital Art, HTML, and CSS

My introduction to web development began with coding simple webpages in the late 2000s. I started developing my design and illustration skills, creating graphics for webpages focusing on my stories and ideas.

September 2014

Attending University

In 2014, I was accepted to Simon Fraser University. I started off studying history, and later switched to biology. While working on my bachelor's degree, I took some introductory programming courses as electives. I wrote my first desktop programs in Python, and later C++.

September 2021

Graduation

I graduated from Simon Fraser University with a degree in ecology. My university studies broadened my perspective of the world, which improved my sense of empathy. In my free time, I developed my design and drawing skills, and built up a portfolio featuring both client and personal work.

September 2023

Attending Camosun College

After about a year, I decided that I needed to change careers. I went back to school at Camosun College, and was accepted to the Interactive Media Developer program. I will continue to build on my knowledge of design and programming.
